# ADR-000: ADR Template

## Status

Template

## Context

Describe the situation or problem that motivates this decision. Include:
- What is the issue we are trying to address?
- What constraints or requirements influence the decision?
- What alternatives were considered?

## Decision

Describe the decision that was made. Include:
- What is the change being made?
- Why was this approach chosen over alternatives?
- What are the key implementation details?

## Consequences

### Positive

- What benefits does this decision provide?
- What becomes easier or more efficient?

### Negative

- What trade-offs or downsides does this decision introduce?
- What becomes harder or more complex?

### Neutral

- What other effects does this decision have?
- Are there any risks or dependencies introduced?

## Related

- Related ADRs: List any related decisions
- Related Issues: Link to relevant GitHub issues
- Related PRs: Link to implementing pull requests
